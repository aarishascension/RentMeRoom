# RentMeRoom - User Guide

## Welcome to RentMeRoom! 🏠

RentMeRoom is a community platform where you can find roommates, share accommodation needs, and connect with people looking for rooms or offering spaces.

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Sign In
- Open the app and you'll see the welcome screen
- Tap the **Google Sign-In button** (colorful G icon) to sign in with your Google account
- Grant permissions when asked
- You're in! 🎉

---

## Main Navigation (Bottom Tabs)

The app has 5 main sections accessible from the bottom navigation bar:

### 🏠 Home Tab
- **What you see**: Feed of all room requests and offers
- **Country Filter**: By default, you only see posts from your country (e.g., 🇮🇳 IN for India)
- **Toggle Global View**: Tap the country badge at the top to switch between:
  - **🇮🇳 IN** - Shows only posts from India
  - **🌍 Global** - Shows posts from all countries
- **Top Icons**:
  - 🗺️ **Map** - View all posts on a map
  - 🔍 **Filter** - Filter by location, price, amenities, etc.
  - 🔔 **Notifications** - Check your notifications

### ➕ Create Post (Center Button)
- **Large circular button** in the center of the bottom bar
- Tap to create a new post about your room needs
- **What to include**:
  - Describe what you're looking for (e.g., "Need 2BHK near Andheri, budget 25k")
  - Add location (auto-detected or manually selected)
  - Add up to 5 photos
  - Your country is auto-detected from GPS
- Tap **Post** when ready

### 💬 Chats Tab
- View all your conversations
- Tap any chat to continue the conversation
- Send messages, photos, and connect with potential roommates

### ⭐ Favorites Tab
- All posts you've liked/favorited appear here
- Quick access to posts you're interested in
- Tap any post to view details

### 👤 Profile Tab
- **Your Profile**: View and edit your name, email, avatar
- **My Posts**: See all posts you've created
- **Settings**: Access app settings and preferences

## Understanding the Home Feed

### Post Cards
Each post shows:
- **User Avatar & Name** - Who posted it
- **Post Description** - What they're looking for
- **Location** 📍 - Where they need accommodation
- **Photos** 📷 - Swipe left/right to see multiple images
- **Engagement Stats**:
  - ❤️ Likes count
  - 💬 Replies count
  - 👁️ Views count

### Interacting with Posts
- **Tap the post** - View full details and replies
- **Tap ❤️ (heart icon)** - Like/unlike the post
- **Tap 💬 (chat icon)** - Start a conversation with the poster
- **Tap ⋮ (three dots)** - Report or block user (if needed)

---

## Post Detail Screen

When you tap on a post, you'll see:

### Top Section
- Full post details with all photos
- User information
- Location on map (if available)
- Action buttons:
  - **💬 Chat** - Message the poster directly
  - **❤️ Like** - Save to favorites
  - **📤 Share** - Share via WhatsApp, SMS, etc.

### Replies Section
- See all comments/replies from other users
- **Add your reply**: Type in the text box at the bottom
- **Send**: Tap the send button to post your reply
- Each reply shows:
  - User who replied
  - Reply text
  - Time posted
  - Option to report (⋮ menu)

---

## Creating a Post - Step by Step

1. **Tap the ➕ button** in the center of the bottom bar
2. **Write your request**: 
   - Example: "Looking for a female roommate for 2BHK in Andheri. Budget 25k. Vegetarian preferred."
3. **Add Location**:
   - Type manually (e.g., "Andheri, Mumbai")
   - Or tap "Select Location" to pick from map
4. **Add Photos** (optional but recommended):
   - Tap "Select Photos"
   - Choose up to 5 photos
   - Reorder using arrow buttons
   - Remove any photo by tapping the X
5. **Review** and tap **Post**
6. Your post is now live! 🎉

## Using Filters

Tap the 🔍 **Filter** icon on the home screen to narrow down posts:

### Available Filters:
- **Location**: Select from major cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, etc.)
- **Property Type**: 1BHK, 2BHK, 3BHK, PG, Flat, etc.
- **Rent Range**: Set minimum and maximum budget
- **Amenities**: WiFi, AC, Parking, Gym, etc.
- **Preferences**: Vegetarian, Non-smoker, Pet-friendly, etc.

Tap **Apply** to see filtered results, or **Clear All** to reset.

## Safety & Community Guidelines

### Report & Block
If you encounter inappropriate content or users:
1. Tap the **⋮ (three dots)** on any post or reply
2. Select **Report** and choose a reason
3. Or select **Block User** to prevent them from contacting you

### Best Practices:
- ✅ Be respectful and courteous
- ✅ Provide accurate information in your posts
- ✅ Meet in public places for initial meetings
- ✅ Verify identity before sharing personal details
- ❌ Don't share sensitive information publicly
- ❌ Don't spam or post irrelevant content
- ❌ Don't harass or abuse other users

## Troubleshooting

### Can't see any posts?
- Check your internet connection
- Try toggling between country-specific and global view
- Pull down to refresh the feed

### Google Sign-In not working?
- Make sure you have a Google account
- Check internet connection
- Try restarting the app

### Location not detected?
- Grant location permissions in phone settings
- Enable GPS
- Try manually entering your location

### Photos not uploading?
- Grant storage/camera permissions
- Check internet connection
- Ensure photos are under 5MB each

### App crashing or slow?
- Clear app cache from phone settings
- Update to the latest version
- Restart your phone
